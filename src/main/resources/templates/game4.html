Diablo® IV is an action RPG that brings players back to the dark and gritty world of Sanctuary.

- The game features an expansive open world where players can explore different regions, fight enemies, and uncover secrets.

- Players can choose from five unique classes: Barbarian, Sorcerer, Druid, Rogue, and Necromancer, each with their own playstyle and abilities.

- Diablo® IV features dynamic events, dungeons, and a vast loot system to keep players engaged for hours on end.
